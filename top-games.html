Top games

The most-played titles in Mexico. Slots lead, but the real picture mixes in crash, instant, and table games — so this list spans all of them. The colored podium marks the top three.

  1. 01

    Gates of Olympus

    Pragmatic Play · Slot · RTP ~96.5%

    Leads the market by a wide margin. A Greek-mythology slot with pays-anywhere symbols, cascading multipliers, very high volatility, and a 5,000× max win.

  2. 02

    Sweet Bonanza

    Pragmatic Play · Slot · max ~21,100×

    The candy-themed favorite that pays by symbol clusters. Preferred by players who like longer sessions with frequent bonus rounds.

  3. 03

    Fortune Tiger

    PG Soft · Slot · "El Tigrito"

    A viral Latin American hit with fast spins and a high hit frequency. One of the most talked-about titles among Mexican players.

  4. 04

    Aviator

    Spribe · Crash · RTP ~97%

    The standout crash game. A multiplier climbs by the second and you cash out before it crashes — fast rounds and a social feel drove its rise from 2023.

  5. 05

    Big Bass Bonanza

    Pragmatic Play · Slot · RTP ~96.7%

    A fishing-themed series that's become a staple across Mexican lobbies, with several sequels in regular rotation.

  6. 06

    Book of Dead

    Play'n GO · Slot · RTP ~96.2%

    An Ancient Egypt adventure following Rich Wilde, prized for its simplicity and the free-spin round that can swing a session fast.

  7. 07

    European Roulette

    Table game · live & virtual

    One of the most popular table games, with 37 numbers (0–36) giving it a lower house edge than other roulette variants.

  8. 08

    Blackjack

    Table game · live & virtual

    A perennial favorite in both virtual and live-dealer formats, rewarding basic strategy with one of the lower house edges in the casino.

  9. 09

    Baccarat (Punto y Banca)

    Table game · live & virtual

    Popular because it needs no complex strategy — you simply back Player, Banker, or Tie and let the cards decide.

  10. 10

    Plinko

    Instant game

    A favorite for instant payouts: drop the ball, pick your risk level, and watch real-time multipliers decide the result.

Also popular: Sugar Rush and Wolf Gold (Pragmatic Play), Starburst and Gonzo's Quest (NetEnt), and Mexican-themed slots at Winpot such as Loco Habanero and Mayan Cache. RTP figures are providers' default values — operators can configure different rates within their regulator's allowed range, so treat them as typical, not guaranteed.
